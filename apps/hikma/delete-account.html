--- layout: page title: Deleting your Account on Hikma Learning App ---

Hikma Learning App respects your right to control your data and account. You can request the deletion of your account at any time.

How to Delete Your Account

Send an email to our support team at: contact.opensourcefeed@gmail.com

Include the following information in your email:

  • Username: Your Hikma Learning App username.
  • Email Address: The email address associated with your account.
  • (Optional) Briefly explain why you're deleting your account. This feedback helps us improve the app experience.

Deletion Timeframe

We strive to delete your account within 3 business days of receiving your request. During periods of high volume, this timeframe may be extended slightly.

Data Deletion

Upon account deletion, we will delete all your personal data associated with your Hikma Learning App account. This includes information such as your username, email address, and any app-specific data you may have created.

Important Information

  • Account deletion is permanent. You will not be able to recover your account or data once it is deleted.
  • If you have any outstanding fees associated with your account, they must be settled before your account deletion request can be processed.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about deleting your account, please don't hesitate to contact our support team at contact.opensourcefeed@gmail.com.
