Thank you for your interest in writing for our blog. If you want to **write for us**, please review these guidelines first. They help keep quality high and the process clear.

![Write Guest Post for Us](/assets/images/post-images/write-for-us.jpg)


## 1. Relevance

* Posts should focus on **open‑source software, Linux, development, or related tech**.
* Posts outside this area may still be published but may not appear on the homepage or main blog feed.


## 2. Quality and Ethics

* Content must be original and useful.
* Avoid spam, false claims, or thin promotional content.
* We do not accept posts about gambling, questionable crypto projects, adult content, or unethical services.
* We may edit or reject any post to maintain quality.


## 3. External Links Policy

* External links will be **do‑follow and indexable by search engines**.
* We do not add sponsored or paid tags to published articles.
* Links must point to relevant and reputable sites.
* Limit: about **one external link per 250 words**.


## 4. Review and Publishing Time

* We usually review posts within **24 hours**.
* We will inform you if the post is accepted, rejected, or needs changes.
* Accepted posts normally go live within **7 days**.



## 5. How Long Posts Stay Live

* Posts stay online for **at least one year**.
* Most remain longer.
* We may remove content if it breaks policy, during rare cleanups, or if the site closes.



## 6. Publishing Fee

* Standard fee: **$20 USD per post**.
* Discounted pricing applies only to existing long‑term partners.
* Payment options include PayPal or supported creator platforms.
* Please complete payment within **one week after publishing** to avoid removal.



## 7. How to Submit

Send your topic or draft by email to: **[contact.opensourcefeed@gmail.com](mailto:contact.opensourcefeed@gmail.com)**

**Subject:** Guest Post Submission

Include:

* Article topic or draft
* Author details
* Planned links (if any)

We will reply as soon as possible.



## FAQ

### Do you accept sponsored posts?

Yes. The publishing fee applies. We do not label posts as sponsored.

### What topics work best?

Open‑source software, Linux, development tools, and related tech topics.

### How many links can I include?

Roughly one external link per 250 words. All links must be relevant.

### How fast will I get a response?

Most submissions receive a reply within 24 hours.



## Final Note

We focus on clear, ethical publishing and long‑term collaboration. These guidelines help keep the process simple for contributors and readers.
